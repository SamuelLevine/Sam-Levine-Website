 While making this website I learned a lot about HTML, CSS, Java, and web development in general. After taking AP Computer Science in my junior year of high school I had acquired a proficient level of knowledge in the java language. This sparked my interest in game development and I created a few games including some of the ones available for download on this site. After learning more about java and a delving into some javascript (which is very similar to java) I felt that I needed a way to share what I know and what I have created. I then decided to try and teach myself the HTML and CSS languages in order to make a website. I began my learning with an HTML and CSS course on codeacademy.com, but I soon got bored of that approach to my learning. I took matters into my own hands and began learning the way many other people do, through youtube videos and forum posts.
