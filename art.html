water droplet


The final for my AP Art History class required us to create a project surrounding one of the works we have learned about this year. I knew I wanted to utilize music, so I decided on the piece "HOWL, eon (I, II)" by Julie Mehretu. While she was painting this piece a pianist was playing music alongside her, from which she drew inspiration. With this I decided to create my own piece on the drums to put together with my rough recreation of her large painting.
