wack

We live in a society
A society where everyone is anxious
A society where everyone has friends
But no one has a friend
A society of social media
Where people only show the best of themselves
Or let their “close friends” know about the worst
A society that frowns upon deviation
That doesn’t want you to pursue what you love
A society that sets expectations for you
Creating this ever growing standard that causes kids to reach places of hopelessness
Society mistreats us
It forces us to embrace it when all we really want to do is shut ourselves off from it

- sam levine

care

I do not cry
It is just something I can’t do
As much as I try
Even if it is for you

I do not cry even when you do so
I am here for you
My hand on your shoulder, heart in tow
I am here for you

I can’t quite comprehend
Why you do not let me help
You say your heart is on the mend
It is something I have never felt

I want to be there for you every step of the way
You say it is best dealt with on your own
Eventually I figure it is okay
That’s when I call you on the phone

You say it does not help for me to worry
It is my choice to worry
I care about you
More than you know

- sam levine

But I can’t

I need help

My stomach hurts

Lonely, just how I like it

How do I do this alone

Solutions are only temporary
Nothing lasts forever

Im not suicidal

fairytale

The most unlikely of fairy tales
That is what we are
That is what I want us to be
But you do not always get what you want
Lessons are learned
People change
Time goes on
I just hope that you stay
It does not need to be a fairytale
That is what I keep telling myself
It does not need to be a fairytale
I will be lucky if anything happens at all
It does not need to be a fairytale…
Let me change
Let time pass
But do not let me leave

- sam levine
